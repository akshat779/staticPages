What
Is Called Art?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art,a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination. The term art encompasses diverse media such as painting, sculpture, printmaking, drawing, decorative arts, photography, and installation.

7 Types of Arts

The seven types of art are painting, sculpture, architecture, photography, printmaking, conceptual art, and performance art Each type has its own style, characteristics, and even artists who made a name for themselves in each of the types. However, the types of art are not limited to the seven mentioned above. There are still other types of art
